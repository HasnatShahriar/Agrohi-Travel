Our Popular Tours

Bangladesh is one of the few countries in South Asia, which remains to be explored. Bangladesh has a delicate and distinctive attraction of its own to offer and it is definitely not a tourist haunt like Nepal or India.

  • Sylhet-The Picture of Nature.Sylhet thus became a district of saints, shrines and daring but virile people.
  • Sundarbans-Home of the Royal Bengal Tiger.Sundarbans is home to many different species of birds, mammals, insects, reptiles and fishes.
  • Dhaka- The Capital of Bangladesh.The capital city Dhaka predominantly was a city of the Mughals.
  • Kuakata-Daughter of the Sea.Kuakata is truly a virgin beach-a sanctuary for migratory winter birds, a series of coconut trees, sandy beach of blue Bay, a feast for the eye.

These are not simply sight-seeing excursions, but real-time learning experiences. Enjoy an ideal blend of adventure and exploration with comfort and relaxation.
